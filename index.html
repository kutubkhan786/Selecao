About

Who we are

A3Pixels provides effective web and e-Learning solutions that are easy to implement and use. Our efforts are directed at consistently building stimulating and state-of-the-art e-Learning products for our clients that makes online learning a fun and enriching experience for the users. Over the years, we have perfected the ability to handle unstructured situations with ease, to give life to ideas and concepts that have never been implemented before, and to manage a multitude of projects without slipping on deadlines. In each project that we undertake, we make every effort to maintain the highest standards of quality and efficiency. A3PIXELS has been profitable in every quarter and has shown a 100% growth in revenues in every successive year since inception.

  • OUR MISSION:

  • To provide our customers timely and cost-effective e-learning solutions
  • Services aligned to their business goals.

WHY CHOOSE US:

At A3Pixels, we believe our business is all about reaching out to people. The e-learning solutions we create are tools that our clients use to reach out to their various audiences, and help them expand their understanding of the product, service or organization. With all the activity that surrounds the business of developing eLearning content, we have realized the most important element is the relationship we build among our clients, their audiences and our people.

Creating elearning content demands understanding the client's perspective on the topic, the learners ' level of comprehension and knowledge and then setting out to bridge the gap.

Our thorough documentation and sharing of our understanding of the project and deliverables; and them using our expertise in communications, design and technology to get the message across plays a key role in creating products that work, are sustainable, scalable and cost-effective. As a services company, we are result oriented. With technical prowess, effective communication, commitment and teamwork, our goal is to become an icon in the online digital industry.

Having said that; 75% of our business comes from existing customers. We have grown with our customers and it makes us proud to state that we have been significant partners in our customers' growth.
